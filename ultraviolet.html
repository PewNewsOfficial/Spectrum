Ultraviolet Waves

Ultraviolet Waves have a 3.4 x 10 to the 16th - 7.9 x 10 to the 14th Frequency and a 3.8 x 10 to the -7th - 8 x 10 to the -9th Meter Wavelength as well as an energy of 0.5 - 20 eV.
These are used in tanning beds, but may give you a nasty sunburn if exposed too long! They are also used by certain animals to see smells and certain chemical trails, which in turn may one day be used by us.
