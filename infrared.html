Infrared Waves

Infrared Waves have a 3.9 x 10 to the fourteenth power - 3 x 10 to the 14th Frequency and a 7.6 x 10 to the negative seventh power - 0.001 Meter Wavelength as well as an energy of 2 x 10 to the negative fourth power - 0.3eV.
They can be used to read heat signatures, which also can allow them to be used as nightvision.
